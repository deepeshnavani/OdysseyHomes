About Igatpuri

At an easy 60-minute drive from Thane (thru the upcoming Samruddhi Mahamarg), this 6-acre land, in Igatpuri, is a blessed escape that’s close to the city but far from its bustle.

With 38 exclusive plots planned to give you absolute privacy and peace, this is a piece of nature you’ll want to own.

When you buy a plot at The Foothills of Bhandardhara, you don’t just buy the privacy of a large plot that can accommodate your farmhouse and any other fancies you wish to build. You buy into a land filled with natural beauty.

Own an exclusive plot of land that can be built to your taste and specification.
